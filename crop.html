Crop Information System

Explore details of various crops, their cultivation, and yields

Wheat

Cultivation Needs: Moderate water, well-drained soil, sunny environment

Growth Pattern: Winter crop, harvested in spring

Location: Grown globally, especially in the USA, China, and India.

Yield:2,500 to 4,000 kg/ha

Maize(corn)

Cultivation Needs: Warm climate, well-drained loamy soil, requires moderate rainfall.

Growth Pattern: Grows in a cycle of 90–120 days.

Location: Majorly in the USA, Brazil, and Argentina.

Yield: 4,000 to 10,000 kg/ha

Soyabean

Cultivation Needs: Well-drained loamy or sandy soil, requires moderate rainfall and warm weather.

Growth Pattern: Grows in a cycle of 80–150 days.

Location: Majorly in the USA, Brazil, and Argentina

Yield: 2,000–4,000 kg/ha

Rice

Cultivation Needs: High water requirement, flooded fields

Growth Pattern: Grows in a cycle of 120–180 days depending on variety.

Location: Predominantly in Asia, particularly in countries like China, India, and Indonesia.

Yield:2,000–3,000 kg/ha

Potato

Cultivation Needs: Cool climates; well-drained, fertile soil; moderate rainfall.

Growth Pattern: Grows in 90–120 days.

Location: Grown worldwide, with high production in China, India, and Russia.

Yield: 20,000–40,000 kg/ha.

Tomato

Cultivation Needs: Warm climate; fertile, well-drained soil with good organic matter.

Growth Pattern: Grows in 75–90 days.

Location: Grown worldwide, especially in the USA, China, and India.

Yield: 25,000–35,000 kg/ha.

Cotton

Cultivation Needs: Warm climate; sandy loam or clay soil; requires irrigation in dry areas

Growth Pattern: Grows in 150–200 days.

Location: U.S., India, China, Pakistan.

Yield: 1,500–2,500 kg/ha.

Peanuts

Cultivation Needs: Requires warm weather, sandy soil, and well-drained conditions

Growth Pattern: Takes about 4-5 months from planting to harvest..

Location: Major production in the USA, China, and India..

Yield: 2-4 tons per hectare.

Sugarcane

Cultivation Needs: Thrives in tropical climates with rich soil and plenty of water.

Growth Pattern: Takes 12-18 months to mature.

Location:Predominantly grown in Brazil, India, and China.

Yield: Average yields are about 70-100 tons per hectare.

Sunflower

Cultivation Needs:Requires full sun, well-drained soil, and moderate water

Growth Pattern: Grows in about 3-4 months.

Location: Grown primarily in Ukraine, Russia, and Argentina.

Yield: Average yields are about 1-3 tons per hectare.

onion

Cultivation Needs: Needs full sun, well-drained soil, and consistent watering.

Growth Pattern: Takes about 3-5 months to mature.

Location: Grown worldwide, with high production in China, India, and the USA.

Yield: Average yields are about 20-30 tons per hectare.

Oats

Cultivation Needs: Prefers cool climates, fertile soil, and adequate rainfall.

Growth Pattern: Takes about 3-4 months to mature.

Location: Commonly grown in Canada, the USA, and northern Europe.

Yield: Approximately 2-4 tons per hectare.
